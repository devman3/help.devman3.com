Issue a tax certificate

Issue a tax certificate from the donation page.

Email it directly to the donor or download it and send from your own email.

Please contact the Support team to set up your template.

Search for the donation you want to issue the tax certifcate for.

On the Donation summary page select Edit

Make sure that Tax certificate is ticked. 

Click on Action, then Tax certificate. 

The donation is now locked and no changes can be made. 

To email the tax certificate directly to the donor from DevMan, click on Email, select the Template, and then Email.

The email has been queued. 

Select View/Download  if you wish to use the following options:

  • print the tax certificate,
  • email it from a different email address,
  • or use any other distribution method you prefer.

Select Download, then Individual files. 

Find your documents in your Downloads folder 

This is an example of a tax certificate issued by DevMan.

The tax certificate number is unique to this donation. 

The tax certificate has been issued.
